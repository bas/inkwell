# Appearance

Inkwell supports light mode, dark mode, and automatic mode that follows the macOS system appearance.

## Changing the colour mode

The **colour mode toggle** is in the bottom-left corner of the app window. It has three options:

| Icon | Option | Behaviour |
|---|---|---|
| ☀️ Sun | **Light** | Always uses the light theme, regardless of your macOS setting. |
| 🖥 Monitor | **Auto** | Follows your macOS *System Settings → Appearance* choice. This is the default. |
| 🌙 Moon | **Dark** | Always uses the dark theme, regardless of your macOS setting. |

Click the icon for the mode you want. Your preference is saved and restored on the next launch.

## Auto mode

When **Auto** is selected, Inkwell switches between light and dark instantly whenever you change the macOS system appearance — no restart required.
